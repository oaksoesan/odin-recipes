Hair Cake

Description: Dive into the bizarre world of Filthy Frank with the Hair Cake, a dessert that challenges conventional tastes. Don't worry; the hair is just spun sugar! It's a feast for the adventurous.

Ingredients

  • Cake Mix
  • Sugar (for hair)
  • Food Coloring (optional)
  • Icing

Steps

  1. Prepare the cake mix according to package instructions.
  2. Create "hair" by melting sugar and pulling it into fine strands. Optionally, add food coloring for a vibrant touch.
  3. Bake the cake and let it cool.
  4. Generously frost the cake with icing, ensuring the "hair" sticks well.
  5. Optional: Style the hair creatively for a unique presentation.
  6. Serve and enjoy Filthy Frank's Hair Cake – a dessert that defies expectations!
