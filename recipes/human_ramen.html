Human Ramen (ヒューマンラーメン)

Description: A whimsical creation featuring a bathtub filled with noodles, garnished with household items, creating the legendary Human Ramen.Dive in for an immersive culinary experience!

Ingredients

  • ヌードル (Nūdoru) - Noodles
  • スープ (Sūpu) - Soup
  • 人間 (Ningen) - Human

Steps

  1. Fill a bathtub with water and add industrial-sized packs of noodles.
  2. Garnish generously with various household items for flavor.
  3. Dive into the concoction and mix well, ensuring an even distribution of noodles and "human" essence.
  4. Serve in a traditional ramen bowl for an authentic touch.
