Vomit Cake Creation

Description: Indulge in the surreal delight of the Vomit Cake, a dessert masterpiece crafted with unconventional ingredients. Baked to perfection, it promises a taste experience that defies traditional culinary norms.

Ingredients

  • Cake Mix
  • Mayonnaise
  • Ketchup
  • Crushed Potato Chips

Steps

  1. Combine cake mix with unconventional ingredients, such as mayonnaise, ketchup, and crushed potato chips.
  2. Stir vigorously until the mixture reaches a texture resembling vomit.
  3. Bake in an oven, ignoring all conventional baking rules.
  4. Once cooled, decorate with additional vomit-like toppings for an extra touch of authenticity.
